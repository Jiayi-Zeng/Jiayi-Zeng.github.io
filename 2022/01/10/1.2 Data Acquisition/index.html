Sources of Popular ML datasets

Source：Web Scraping，Collect

  • MNIST

    Digital written

  • ImageNet

    millions of images from image search engines

  • Audio Set

    YouTube sound clips for sound classification

  • Kinetics

    YouTube videos clips for human actions classification

  • KITTI

    Traffic scenarios(场景) recorded by cameras and other sensors

  • SQuAD

    Question-answer pairs derived from Wikipedia

  • Amazon Review

    Customer reviews and from Amazon

  • LibriSpeech

    1000 hours of audiobooks

Where to find datasets 数据集搜索途径

  • Paperswithcodes Datasets 论文数据集

    Academic datasets with leaderboard

  • Kaggle Datasets 常用

    ML datasets uploaded by data scientists

  • Google Dataset search 搜索引擎

    Search datasets in the Web

  • TensorFlow, hugging face

    Various toolkits(工具包) datasets

  • Various conference/ company ML competitions

  • Open Data on AWS

    100+ large-scale raw data (P级别）

  • Data lakes(数据湖) in your own organization

Datasets comparison 数据集对比

Pro 优点 Cons 缺点
Academic datasets Clean, proper difficulty Limited choices, too simplified, usually small scale. 不适合做产品、做应用，适合算法测试
Competition datasets Closer to real ML applications Still simplified, and only available for hot topics
Raw Data Great flexibility Needs a lot of efforts to process
  • Deal with raw data in industrial settings
  • Data curation(管理) can be a big projection involving multiple teams
  • Processing pipeline，storage， legal issue，privacy……
